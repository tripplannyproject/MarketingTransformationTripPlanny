# Diseñar marketing B2B2C

## Inputs obligatorios

- Partner types/tiers, end-user audience, agreements, markets y channel conflict rules.
- Brand ownership, MDF/fund rules, lead routing, data sharing y success metrics.

## Procedimiento

1. Separar partner journey de end-user journey y conectar sus outcomes.
2. Definir value exchange, enablement, campaign-in-a-box y local flexibility.
3. Mapear brand/asset ownership, approvals y co-brand hierarchy.
4. Diseñar MDF request/use/proof, routing y attribution.
5. Medir partner activation, sourced pipeline, sell-through y adoption.

## Salida auditable

Producir `partner-marketing-plan` con tiers, plays, rights, funds, routing y metrics.

## Límites y gates

No usar partner brand/data/funds fuera del agreement ni atribuir end-user consent al partner implícitamente.
