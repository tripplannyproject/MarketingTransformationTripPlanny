# Diseñar UGC y creator marketing

## Inputs obligatorios

- Campaign objective, audience, creator fit, markets, platform y compensation model.
- Rights/consent, disclosure rules, claims y brand safety criteria.

## Procedimiento

1. Definir creator/UGC role, authenticity criteria y selection rubric.
2. Crear brief con message guardrails, proof y creative freedom.
3. Negociar conceptualmente channels, term, territories, edits, likeness y paid usage.
4. Definir disclosure, moderation, approvals y takedown process.
5. Diseñar measurement que separe content, distribution y commercial effect.

## Salida auditable

Producir `creator-program-brief` y rights checklist por creator/asset.

## Límites y gates

No fabricar UGC/testimonials ni ocultar relación comercial. No usar likeness/content sin permiso firmado.
