# Diseñar demand generation y ABM

## Inputs obligatorios

- ICP, accounts permitidas, buying roles, offer, sales process y pipeline baseline.
- Consent/data sources, channels, capacity, regions y revenue definitions.

## Procedimiento

1. Segmentar account tiers y buying committee por need/fit, no solo firmographics.
2. Mapear questions, proof y content por role/stage.
3. Elegir 1:1, 1:few o 1:many con trade-offs/capacity.
4. Diseñar orchestration marketing-sales, routing y SLA.
5. Definir leading, pipeline y revenue metrics con attribution caveats.

## Salida auditable

Producir `b2b-demand-plan` con ICP, account rules, plays, content, handoffs y metrics.

## Límites y gates

No enriquecer personas, exportar listas ni iniciar outreach sin audience privacy y activation gate.
