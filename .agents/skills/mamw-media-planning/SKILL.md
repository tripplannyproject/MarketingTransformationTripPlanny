# Planear paid media

## Inputs obligatorios

- Objective, audience/market, funnel, creative capability y approved budget ceiling.
- Platform profiles, account/tracking readiness, benchmarks y historical evidence.

## Procedimiento

1. Comparar platforms por objective, audience, intent/context, format y readiness.
2. Descartar cuentas/tracking no verificadas y explicar trade-offs.
3. Asignar budget dentro del ceiling con base/upside/downside scenarios.
4. Definir flighting, pacing, frequency, bidding assumptions y creative matrix.
5. Diseñar measurement, experiments y stop conditions.

## Salida auditable

Producir `platform-selection` y `media-plan-v1` con evidence, confidence, allocations y risks.

## Límites y gates

No prometer results ni cambiar el techo upstream. No crear/activar campañas desde esta skill.
