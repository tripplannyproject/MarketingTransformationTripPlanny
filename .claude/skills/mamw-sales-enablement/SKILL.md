# Crear sales enablement

## Inputs obligatorios

- ICP/roles, product truth, proof, approved claims, sales stages y objections reales.
- Competitive evidence, locale, channels y content owners.

## Procedimiento

1. Priorizar decisiones y preguntas del buyer por stage.
2. Mapear value/proof, discovery prompts y objections con evidencia.
3. Crear asset adecuado: battlecard, one-pager, deck narrative o follow-up.
4. Alinear terminology, pricing scope y handoff con PAMW/sales.
5. Versionar expiry y feedback loop con field usage.

## Salida auditable

Producir `sales-enablement-package` con audience, stage, claim refs, sources y owner.

## Límites y gates

No inventar competitor facts, roadmap, customer logos, pricing ni case results.
